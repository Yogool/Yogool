The Problem with Traditional AI Search

Current AI search often treats everything found online as equally valid, leading to inaccurate or irrelevant summaries.

Yogool's Solution: Precision & Relevance

Unlike others, Yogool doesn't just crawl; it *curates*. Our advanced RankWell algorithm rigorously assesses the quality and relevance of sources before synthesizing information, ensuring you get reliable answers every time.

How Yogool Works: The RankWell Algorithm

Our core innovation is RankWell, an algorithm designed to prioritize genuinely valuable web pages.

Instead of just looking at keywords or popularity, RankWell focuses on a crucial signal of credibility: the quality of outbound links.

RankWell in Action:

Imagine two pages, Page A and Page B, both with 18 links.

  • Page A: 12 links point to high-quality, reputable sources.
  • Page B: Only 6 links point to high-quality sources.

Result: Yogool's RankWell ranks Page A higher because its authors demonstrate a commitment to referencing credible information, signaling higher trustworthiness and relevance for your queries.

This filtered, high-quality information is then sent to our advanced LLM to generate accurate, concise, and conversational summaries.
